## Why CD Matters for Agile Teams

- Agile teams deliver in short iterations - CD ensures every increment can be released to users quickly and safely
- Automated deployments remove manual steps, reducing human error and keeping feedback loops short
- CD makes it easy to roll back a bad release, so teams can experiment and iterate with confidence
